How to Create a Childcare Account in the UK

What is a Childcare Account?

A childcare account is an online service provided by the UK government to help parents and guardians manage childcare benefits such as Tax-Free Childcare and 30 hours of free childcare. Creating an account is simple and gives you direct access to manage funds and applications online.

Steps to Create Your Childcare Account

  1. Visit the GOV.UK Childcare Account page.
  2. Click on the option to "Create an account" or "Register" if you are new.
  3. Provide your personal details, including:
    • Your name and address.
    • National Insurance number.
    • Date of birth.
  4. Set up a Government Gateway account by creating a user ID and password.
  5. Verify your identity by answering security questions or using HMRCâ€™s recommended verification methods.
  6. Once verified, you will gain access to your childcare account dashboard.

Need Help?

If you encounter any difficulties while creating your childcare account, visit the GOV.UK Help Centre or contact HMRC for further assistance.
