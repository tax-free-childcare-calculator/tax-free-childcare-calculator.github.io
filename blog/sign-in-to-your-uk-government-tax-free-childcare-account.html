Sign In to Your Childcare Account

About Your Childcare Account

The UK government offers a childcare account to help parents and guardians manage benefits such as Tax-Free Childcare and 30 hours of free childcare. This guide explains how to sign in and navigate your account effectively.

Steps to Sign In

  1. Visit the official GOV.UK childcare account page.
  2. Click on the "Sign in" button located prominently on the page.
  3. Enter your Government Gateway user ID and password. If you don't have one, you can create an account during the process.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to verify your identity if signing in for the first time.
  5. Access your childcare account dashboard to manage payments, check eligibility, and apply for benefits.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you face challenges signing in, here are some tips:

  • Forgotten Password: Use the "Forgot your password?" link on the sign-in page to reset your password.
  • Account Lockout: If your account is locked after multiple failed attempts, wait for 24 hours or contact HMRC support.
  • Technical Issues: Ensure your browser is updated and try clearing your cache or cookies.

Need Help?

For further assistance, visit the GOV.UK Help Centre or contact HMRC support for technical and account-related queries.
