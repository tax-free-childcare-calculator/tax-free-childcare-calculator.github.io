What You Can Use Tax-Free Childcare For

Approved Childcare Services

Tax-Free Childcare can be used to pay for a variety of approved childcare services. These include:

  • Childminders
  • Nurseries
  • Nannies
  • After-school clubs
  • Play schemes

To use Tax-Free Childcare funds, your childcare provider must be registered with the scheme. Make sure to check with your provider to confirm they are signed up before making any payments.

For Families with Disabled Children

If your child is disabled, the benefits of Tax-Free Childcare extend even further:

  • You can use the additional funds to pay for extra hours of childcare.
  • The money can also help your childcare provider purchase specialist equipment for your child, such as mobility aids.

Speak with your childcare provider to explore the available options for specialist equipment that can support your child’s needs.

Important Notes

Tax-Free Childcare is a flexible scheme designed to make childcare more affordable for families. However, it is essential to confirm the following:

  • Your childcare provider is approved and registered under the scheme.
  • You are aware of the expenses that qualify for Tax-Free Childcare benefits.

How to Get Started

If you haven’t already set up a Tax-Free Childcare account, visit the official GOV.UK Tax-Free Childcare page to apply. Once approved, you can start using the funds for eligible childcare services.
